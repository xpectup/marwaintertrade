About Us

Marwa Inter Trade

Export & Import
We are an Indian Exporter, Import, and Wholesaler of all types of Indian Jewellery, Garments, and Footwear like Indian Fashion Jewelry, Indian Kundan Jewelry, Polki Jewelry, men's, women's, children's Dress, and Fashion Footwear.

1.    MarwaIntertrade, specializes in the Importing & Exporting of precious and semi-precious gemstones, watches, garments, leather accessories, machinery, etc.
2.    All our products are of the finest quality, thus we guarantee absolute customer satisfaction.
3.    We are pleased to introduce ourselves to the market, as we are in this business for the past two decades and have successfully taken our business to international markets.
4.    We started our global business operation in the year 2009. Within a short period, we have grown considerably as one of the leading Importer and Exporter.
5.    Our company enjoys a good reputation among all our customers in the local and International markets.
6.    The company also offers its expertise and connections in sourcing superior fine-quality gemstones from local and international sources.
